MOV

mov instruction copies data from source location to destination.

push instruction places its operand onto the top of stack.

POP

pop instruction removes 4-byte data from the top of stack. (对比push)

JMP

jmp instruction transfers program control flow to the instruction at the memory location indicated by the operand.
