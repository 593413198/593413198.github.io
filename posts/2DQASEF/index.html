Instructions: 指令集

阅读如下文档:

  • x86 Assembly Guide
Instruction Usage Example
MOV copy data from source to destination. mov eax, ebx
PUSH place data onto stack top. push eax
POP remove 4-byte data froms tack top and save. pop edi
JMP transfer PC to somewhere. jmp begin
  • Logic Instruction
    ADD, SUB, DIV, OR ...

怎么实现这些指令集?

以PUSH为例, 即将操作数(假设为寄存器ax上的数据)压入栈顶, 那么分为两步实现:

  • 1.退栈: --sp
  • 2.入栈: *sp = ax;
